About

Nicole Marcks has performed across the country onstage with various regional theaters as well as in New York and Los Angeles. She’s also appeared onscreen in award winning independent movies and on TV for Universal/ NBC and HBO.

Having studied improv with The Groundlings and Bang Comedy Theatre the actress found her true calling as a performer through improvisation. Nicole played with various groups in LA and taught improv at Bang. Performing as a regular with the wildly popular group, Totally Looped, Nicole played alongside seasoned comedians and improvisers such as Dan Castellaneta, Maria Bamford, Laraine Newman, Rick Overton and Keegan-Michael Key.

Currently Nicole lives in Park City, Utah, where she is a partner with Triple I Creative; a full service improv corporate training and performance company, and often performs as a founding member in the improv group Park City Improv / Hot Toddy and with Salt Lake troupes Laughing Stock and Quick Wits. She's a graduate of the Juilliard Drama Division.
